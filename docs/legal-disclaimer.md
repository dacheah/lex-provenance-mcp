# Legal disclaimer

**This is research and reference infrastructure. It is not legal advice.**

**Neutral record.** This corpus records what legal instruments say. It takes no legal position, expresses no view on the law or on any live matter, institution, or dispute, and does not represent the legal position of the maintainer or any other party.

- Nothing returned by these tools is a legal conclusion, opinion, or advice, and nothing here creates
  a lawyer–client relationship.
- The connector returns *retrieved source text and citations*. It does not interpret the law, apply it
  to facts, or tell you what to do.
- Always verify any text against the cited official source before relying on it. Use the
  `content_sha256` and `source_url` in each response to do so.
- Legal texts change. A citation is pinned to a dated version; newer versions may exist. Check the
  canonical corpus and the official source for the current state of the law.
- Coverage is partial (this repo ships samples) and provided "as is", without warranty of any kind, to
  the fullest extent permitted by law.
- Authoritative status and fidelity flags are provided honestly but may contain errors; the official
  source always governs.
- **Verification status ([verify]).** Each text carries a fidelity flag (`extracted_verified`, `extracted_unverified`, `ocr_unverified`). Treat anything not `extracted_verified` as **[verify]** - to be independently checked against the official source before it is relied on, and to be put to a reviewing lawyer where certainty is required.

If you need legal advice, consult a qualified lawyer in the relevant jurisdiction.
